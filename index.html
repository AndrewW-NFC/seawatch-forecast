Is today a good day to watch seabirds from shore?

Pick a place. The forecast gives three plain answers: whether birds may be close, whether you can see well, and what gear will help.

Shadmoor State Park

Worth trying this morning.

This is a mostly one-direction seawatch. You stand on the south shore and scan the ocean to the southeast.

Will birds be close?

Maybe. The wind is coming from the water toward land, which can bring seabirds closer than usual.

Good sign

Can I see far?

Somewhat. The air is clear enough, but waves and glare may make faraway birds harder to identify.

Mixed

What should I bring?

Bring a spotting scope if possible. Binoculars should still help for large, closer birds.

Scope helpful
